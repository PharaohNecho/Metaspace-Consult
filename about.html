Our Story

Our Philosophy

In a dynamic and fast-paced world where strategies become outdated quickly, we forecast trends ahead of time and offer innovative solutions to clients to be ahead of other competitors. This is done by Fostering cultural changes and a “digital at the core” mindset. We crowdsource, co-create and pilot new ideas and business models among different teams. Large companies that are not digital natives often find it difficult to replace legacy structures and processes with digital-first approaches. We assist these companies by improving the performances of employees through human capital development, optimizing operations, fueling digital growth, and promoting a culture that drives innovation which in turn creates a sustainable change in society.

Our Vision

To promote a culture that drives transformation and innovation levaraging Technology by organizing and inspring young innovative talents that will craete a sustainable change in their societies.

Our Mission

Improving performances, optimizing operations, fueling digital growth and promoting a culture that drives innovation which in turn creates a sustainable change in the society.

YOB Nation

The YOB Nation is an ecosystem of change-makers that brings together experts, founders, individuals, non-profits around Africa to make a difference through digital transformation. It creates opportunities for companies, NGOs to network and collaborate; hence igniting a quest for a better Africa Society. With YOB Nation, everyone can be a change-maker, employees, won't see themselves as employees, rather change-makers, founders creating people-centric solutions, NGOs working with private sectors to drive change, parents teaching their children to be change-makers. All these can be made possible with technology.
